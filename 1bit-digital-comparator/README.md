## Description

This directory contains materials and documentation for the 1-Bit Digital Comparator physics practical experiment conducted at Tri-Chandra Campus, Tribhuvan University.

## Contents

- Circuit diagrams
- Experimental procedures
- Data and observations

## Purpose

The 1-Bit Digital Comparator is a fundamental digital logic circuit that compares two 1-bit binary inputs and produces outputs indicating their relationship (equal, greater than, or less than).
